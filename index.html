What We Believe

Voters deserve representation, and to be able to support their congressional representatives based on how they actually behave on specific issues over time.

In the current political environment, members of the US House of Representatives and US Senate who do a good job acting out the will of their constituents should not have to spend so much of their time fundraising instead of legislating.

There are a lot of problems in the American political system and simple, creative, transparent solutions can begin to improve things.

What We Intend To Do About It

We are building a mobile app that will:

  • Allow voters to
    • Tell their congressional representatives how they feel on specific bills before votes
    • Encourage representative decision-making and actions
    • Help representatives spend less time raising funds among big donors
  • Enable congressional representatives to
    • Raise funds for party obligations and campaign funds by voting with constituents
    • Access real-time polling on how constituents feel about specific bills
    • Meaningfully connect with voters about issues with digital communications tools
