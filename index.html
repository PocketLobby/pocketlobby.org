Pocket Lobby

Pocket Lobby is a free app that provides simple explanations of what Congress is voting on, shows you how well your Senators and Congressional Representative are voting for you, and lets you cover their debts when they do a good job.

Whether you're on the right, the left, or somewhere in between - welcome. We're glad you found us.

We Shouldn't Need This App

But we do.

Regular people have the right to be heard in D.C, but we aren't and we know why. Even though there are issues that most of us agree on, we don't see action because a few people who have a lot of money disagree with the rest of us – right now, that money is more valuable to our leaders than our decisions. Pocket Lobby lets you see how your person in Congress is voting and lets you give money to cover the debts of your Senators and Representatives when they vote the way you want them to on specific issues.

We believe that most politicians don't have truly clean hands – right and left – but not all of them are criminal, and most of them would prefer another fundraising option.

We intend to provide your elected officials with better data about how you want them to vote and an alternative to Big Money donations – so that you can hold any politician representing you, who still chooses to continue accepting Big Money, accountable at the ballot box. How?

We are building a mobile app that will:

  • Allow voters to
    • Tell their congressional representatives how they feel on specific bills before votes
    • Encourage representative decision-making and actions
    • Help representatives spend less time raising funds among big donors
  • Enable congressional representatives to
    • Raise funds for party obligations by voting with constituents
    • Access real-time polling on how constituents feel about specific bills
    • Meaningfully connect with voters about issues with digital communications tools
